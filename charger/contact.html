Our Story

Founded by Smith Baggett, a fourth-generation cattle rancher from Pulaski, Georgia, Flamingo Charger was born out of necessity. Frustrated by bulky power banks, Baggett created the FlaminGO Charger, which landed a deal with Barbara Corcoran on Shark Tank Season 16 for $100,000 in exchange for 22% equity.

Our Mission

To provide innovative, portable charging solutions that keep tech enthusiasts and travelers connected worldwide.
